Angela Davis

Angela davis was born on January 6th, 1944 in Birmingham, Alabama. She is an American political activist, author, and professor at the University of California, Santa Cruz. She was an active member in the Black Panther Party and the communist party. After coming out as a lesbian in 1977, Davis has continued to tackle oppression faced by the black community, women, and the LGBTQ+ community. She is the author of more than ten books on class, gender, race, and the U.S. prison system.
